Ruby Velle and the Soulphonics

Ruby Velle and the Soulphonics were united in 2006 at their Gainesville, Florida hometown. The team started with Spencer Garn (keys), Scott Clayton (guitar), and Ruby Velle (vocals) but has since expanded to include drum, bass, and brass instruments. Their vintage R&B-Soul-Funk Fusion pays homage to the old-school masters with extra inspiration from Incubus, Portishead, and Ani DiFranco. Through their showing in works like “It’s About Time” they quickly found a home in Atlanta and solidified themselves as an inspirational local band. View Live Performance


Björk

Björk is a legend in the music scene. She’s 58 years old hailing from Reykjavik, Iceland and known for her innovative and eclectic music style. While she blends various genres she is most known for her Art Pop and Electronic style full of experimentation and unique singing. She consistently produces visually striking music videos and thought-provoking performances. Practically all of her albums have received critical acclaim, though her LP "Homogenic" is widely considered her magnum opus. View Live Performance


Tumi Mogorosi

Tumi Mogorosi is a 37 year old drummer from Johannesburg, South Africa. He is celebrated for his contributions to contemporary South African jazz, while also being a composer and activist on the side. He is a member of Shabaka & the Ancestors, but also leads his own ensemble. Be sure to check out his superb album “Group Theory”! View Live Performance


Samaria

Samaria is an upcoming 28 years old artist born in Oakland, California. She started her music career in 2016 and has since grown into her signature Soul and Alternative R&B sound. She takes influences from classic and modern styles and it definitely shows in her project "Reckless". We can't wait to hear what else she has in store! View Live Performance
