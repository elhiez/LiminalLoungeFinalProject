#2. Are reservations required to attend?

Reservations are recommended but not required. We operate on a first-come, first-serve basis. However, for larger groups or special events, we highly appreciate guests making a reservation in advance so we can accomodate you.

#3. Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. Contactless payment options like Apple Pay are also available for your convenience.

#4. Is it possible to host a private event at Liminal?

Yes! We offer options for private events and parties. Please contact us directly at info@liminalloungeatl.com for more details. Afterwards we will discuss your event, the guests attending, any accomodations necessary, etc. Please do the same if you wish to cancel or postpone the event.

#5. What are the policies set in place for COVID-19?

  • Health and Safety Guidelines:

    • To ensure the safety and well-being of our guests and staff, we follow all recommended health and safety protocols, including regular sanitation of surfaces, social distancing measures, and encouraging mask-wearing, especially when sick. We also provide hand sanitizing stations and wet wipes throughout the venue for your convenience. Finally, we highly encourage you to stay home if you feel unwell or have been in contact with someone who has tested positive for COVID-19. #6. Are there age restrictions for entry? Where do I go to learn more about the venue's accessibility?

Yes, in order to enjoy the restaurant and live music you must be an adult (18 years or older). In order to enter the bar, you must be 21 years of age or older. As always, a valid ID is required to purchase alcoholic beverages. The Liminal venue does have elevators and is wheelchair accessible, but if you have any more questions, whether it be general or specific (job hirings, venue layout, etc.), feel free to call us at (404) 555-7834, or email us at info@liminalloungeatl.com.
