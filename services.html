Optional Experience Packages

Tier 1. Pampered Regular ($75/month)

Benefits:


  • Enjoy a 10% discount on all food and beverages.
  • Earn loyalty points with every 5 purchases from Liminal Lounge which can be redeemed for future discounts and special perks.
  • Receive two complimentary drinks or meals each month from our special selection.

Tier 2. VIP ($150/month)

Benefits:


  • Enjoy a 20% discount on all food and beverages.
  • Earn loyalty points with every 3 purchases from Liminal Lounge which can be redeemed for future discounts and special perks.
  • Receive four complimentary drinks or meals each month from our special selection.
  • Receive access to priority seating and the VIP lounge where you can try new additions to our menu early.

Tier 3. MVP ($300/month)

Benefits:


  • Enjoy a 30% discount on all food and beverages.
  • Earn loyalty points with every other purchase from Liminal Lounge which can be redeemed for future discounts and special perks.
  • Receive six complimentary drinks or meals each month from our special selection.
  • Receive guaranteed access to front house seating and the MVP lounge where you can try new additions to our menu early at no added cost. You may also enjoy the extra amenities and feedback service put in place to influence Liminal's next move for guest artists and menu items.
  • Enjoy our free valet parking service for convenience and ease of access at any time.
