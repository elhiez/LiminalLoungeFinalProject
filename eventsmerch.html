A Look Back at Guests

Thanks to your support we can regularly highlight popular, upcoming, and internationally renowned artists. Take a look at some of our most exciting guests from the past!

Sade Adu

R&B Singer

Sade Adu is a British-Nigerian singer songwriter renowned for her smooth, soulful voice and timeless hits such as "Smooth Operator", "No Ordinary Love", and “Like a Tattoo”. Her music blends elements of soul, jazz, and soft rock with next level elegance and emotion. Check out a live performance here.

Kamasi Washington

Jazz Musician

Kamasi Washington is an American jazz saxophonist, composer, bandleader, and pioneer known for his expansive works that push the boundaries of contemporary jazz. His critically acclaimed album "The Epic" brought jazz to a new generation, incorporating elements of hip-hop, classical, and world music in a renaissance of sorts. Check out a live performance here.

Lisa Ono

Bossa Nova Artist

Lisa Ono is a Japanese-Brazilian bossa nova singer and guitarist celebrated for her delicate voice and authentic interpretations of popular Brazilian music that are sure to make you smile. She’s played a significant role in popularizing bossa nova around the world, blending traditional Brazilian rhythms with her warm, comforting singing style. She also sings in several other languages, including Spanish, French, and English. Check out a live performance here.

Portishead

Trip-Hop Group

Portishead is an English band from Bristol known for their dark, atmospheric soundscapes and haunting melodies. Fronted by Beth Gibbons, the band makes great use of airy existentialist vocals mixed in with an innovative use of sampling, turntables, and live instrumentation to create classics like "Glory Box" and “Mysterons”. Their music--primarily characterized by its distinct moody and cinematic quality--continues to inspire artists today, even with the band members having gone their separate ways in recent times. Check out a live performance here.

Carlos Simon

Classical Music Composer

Carlos Simon is a notorious American composer and musician known for his theatrical, socially conscious compositions that take music a step further. Through his hard work and first-hand experience as a black man living in America, Simon has created a platform to open global discussion on themes of history, culture, and human experience simply through sound and instrumentation. In our eyes, he's one of the most powerful activists in our age of modern classical music, even if he prefers to work more behind the scenes. Check out some of his pieces here.

Ichiko Aoba

Folk Artist

Ichiko Aoba is a Japanese singer-songwriter and guitarist who plays around with a delicate, minimalistic folk production style. Her lyrics often explore themes of spirituality and self reflection which fit perfectly with her soft, ethereal voice and laid back acoustic guitar playing. When you listen to Ichiko you’re guaranteed to be transported to a beautiful flowing field of emotional serenity. See what we mean for yourself.
